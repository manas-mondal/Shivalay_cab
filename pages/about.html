Who We Are

West Bengal's Most Trusted Travel Partner

YatraWheels was founded with one simple belief — every journey deserves comfort, reliability, and a smile. Since 2010, we have been the preferred car rental and hotel assistance partner for thousands of families, business travellers, and tourists exploring West Bengal's beautiful destinations.

From the misty hills of Darjeeling to the golden beaches of Digha, from the mangroves of Sundarbans to the heritage of Murshidabad — our experienced drivers and carefully maintained fleet ensure you travel safely and comfortably, every single time.

We don't just provide vehicles — we plan journeys. Our team helps you choose the right car, recommends the best hotels along your route, and stays available round the clock through a simple call or WhatsApp.

Licensed, verified and background-checked drivers with 5+ years of road experience
Well-maintained fleet of sedans, SUVs, and Tempo Travellers for all group sizes
Handpicked hotel partnerships from budget stays to luxury resorts across the state
24/7 customer support — reachable via call or WhatsApp at any hour

Our Mission & Vision

The values and goals that guide every journey we undertake

Our Mission

To make travel across West Bengal safe, affordable, and genuinely enjoyable for every passenger. We are committed to providing punctual service, courteous drivers, and transparent pricing — so you can focus entirely on your journey, not the logistics.

We aim to be the first call every traveller makes — whether it's a morning airport pickup, a family hill station trip, or a corporate outstation tour.

Our Vision

To become West Bengal's most loved and most trusted end-to-end travel service — connecting people to destinations with comfort, reliability, and care. We envision a network of verified drivers and premium hotel partners spanning every corner of the state.

Our long-term goal is to make quality travel accessible to all — from first-time tourists to seasoned business travellers — without compromising on safety or service.
